Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
